## PSU MacAdmins Conference 🎤

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of presenting at the **PSU MacAdmins Conference**, sharing workflows, automation strategies, and real-world approaches to managing Apple devices at scale.

Below are the sessions I’ve presented:

---

### 2025 - *Leverage the Air in your Mosyle iPad Deployments*

Focuses on strategies and tools for managing large-scale iPad deployments in K–12 schools using Mosyle OneK12 MDM. I cover:
  * Gateway School District’s setup: 3,200 students, 600 staff, 1:1 iPads in K–8, Chromebooks in high school, and a student-led help desk.
  * Homegrown tools: Bash, PowerShell, and PHP scripts to automate iPad management (e.g., MOSBasic, ShakeNBake, Loaner Station, and Musky web interfaces).
  * Deployment techniques: “Limbo” setup for preloading essential apps, Return To Service (RTS) workflows, and fast device provisioning.
  * Use of AI (ChatGPT): For coding assistance, building tools like Musky, and even recreating legacy logos.
  * Musky platform: A web-based interface for streamlining help desk support, lost mode, device wiping, and custom module integration.
  * Future roadmap: Enhancing class-level visibility, documentation, modular flexibility, and support for more student and teacher workflows.

Overall, I try to explain and demonstrate how creative scripting, automation, and AI can simplify iPad fleet management and empower student help desks in a Mosyle-managed environment.

  * Presentation -> https://youtu.be/9nEsrAa3JaE

### 2022 – *iPad Deployments Using Mosyle Manager and Custom Tools*
An overview of the district's migration from JAMF Pro to Mosyle Manager in 2020. The presentation detailed their use of Mosyle's "Limbo mode" for pre-configuration, managing device breakage and loaners, and introduced MOSBasic, a custom command-line tool he developed to automate tasks via the Mosyle API. It also covered using CFGUtil to efficiently re-image and reprovision iPads.

This presetation was done during the Campfire Session Years.  My first Virtual/Remote presentation done with my co-presenter Jennifer Czyzewski

### 2019 – *iPad Evolution at Gateway School District*
 A case study of Gateway School District's iPad deployments, detailing the mistakes from their initial 1:1 rollout in 2014 and the lessons applied to their successful 2017 redeployment. The talk covered their evolution to K-8 1:1 and large-scale Shared iPad deployments in elementary schools, including topics like Apple TV deployment and using the JAMF Pro API
